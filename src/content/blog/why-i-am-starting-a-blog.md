As part of my wish to grow as both a developer and an individual, I have come to the conclusion that I need to share my experiences/knowledge.

This conclusion was reached when I realized I need to articulate what I am doing better since it has become a hinderance to my progress as an individual.

The blog posts will be frequent and will be interesting.

I pray that I can keep going, keep learning, keep engaging with others and provide even the smallest bit of knowledge to whoever comes across this site.

## Antony Nyagah - Backend Developer and Associate Data Scientist

**Greetings!** I'm Antony Nyagah, a lover of tech mainly code. My goal as of now is to provide solutions to problems people face.

## Skills and Expertise

I specialize in back-end development mostly with **Python** but I have also been learning **Go**.  
I have gotten into the **Data Science** space thanks to my love for visualizations 📊. In regards to certifications I am an [Associate Data Scientist](https://www.datacamp.com/certificate/DSA0011358609209) certified by DataCamp.

## Innovation and Problem-Solving

I thrive on solving problems with actual impact to the society. This might be one of the reasons that I am thankful to have worked at [HuQAS](https://www.huqas.org/), an external quality assessment provider. They essentially make sure lab results from hospitals/clinics are accurate and correct. I believe I have saved a life or two 😼.
